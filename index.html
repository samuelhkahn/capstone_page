Benchmarks

We train our machine learning model on a set of labels generated by existing ad-blocking filter lists, so our baseline is accuracy at reproducing the hand-curated filters. Performance during day-to-day use may sometimes exceed that of existing ad blockers by extrapolating from known cases in ways that hand-written filters are unable to do.
